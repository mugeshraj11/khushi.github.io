Who are we?

‘KHUSHI’.. true to its name and commitment to bring happiness in the lives of the people is bringing people (read psychologist, psychiatrists, counsellors etc) and entities who are committed to transform your lives which will put you in a mind space filled with positivity, excitement and looking-forward to great things that life has to offer. The platform provides a great opportunity to seek professional help from experts who have transformed thousands of the lives. The platform further takes it one step ahead by connecting you with life style improving entities who will make your life interesting, adventurous & healthy. Interaction with our experts will be completely confidential. A report of the interaction will be generated after each session. The reports will be pin protected so no one will have access other than the person. So Register now !!
